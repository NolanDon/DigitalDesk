About Us

Hi, I'm Nolan Boxill. A few years ago, I found myself frustrated with the corporate grind. The long hours, the lack of flexibility, and the feeling of being just another cog in the machine. I decided to take control of my life and pursue a path that truly excites me—freelance web design.

What really drove me to this new journey was the lack of support and affordable options for small businesses when it comes to their websites. I saw so many small businesses struggling to get their online presence right, either unable to afford the high upfront costs or getting stuck with poor-quality websites that didn't help them grow.

That's when I decided to make a change. I started offering affordable, custom web design solutions to small businesses that need a professional online presence without breaking the bank.

Our Streamlined Process

Your journey to a custom, high-performing website is straightforward. From gathering essential details to bringing your vision to life, we handle each step efficiently, aiming for a quick, smooth launch within 2-3 weeks.

Step 1

Quick Business Insight

We begin by sending you a short questionnaire to capture the essence of your business—what you offer, your unique strengths, and more. We’ll take care of transforming your answers into impactful content.

Step 2

Design Walkthrough & Feedback

We present a first draft of your website in a screen-share meeting, guiding you through the design and gathering your input to ensure it aligns with your vision.

Step 3

Build & Launch

With your approval, we proceed with development. Once the site is ready, you’ll receive a demo link to review before we officially launch, ensuring a perfect start.
