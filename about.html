About

Hi, I'm Nolan Boxill. Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working as a software developer for companies of all sizes—from billion-dollar enterprises to nimble startups—and even managing sensitive financial data for highly regulated industries. As a proven technical leader, I’ve always valued transparency, trust, and clear communication. A few years ago, I realized that the corporate grind wasn’t for me. The long hours, rigid structures, and lack of personal connection left me unfulfilled. I wanted more flexibility and the opportunity to create something meaningful. That’s when I decided to take a leap and pursue a career in freelance web design. What truly inspired me was seeing the challenges small businesses face in building an effective online presence. Too often, they’re met with high upfront costs, subpar websites, or a lack of ongoing support. I wanted to change that. By offering affordable, custom web design solutions, I’ve made it my mission to help small businesses establish a professional online presence that drives growth—without breaking the bank. With every project, I bring not just technical expertise, but also a commitment to empowering my clients through open communication and designs that truly reflect their vision.

Our Streamlined Process

Your journey to a custom, high-performing website is straightforward. From gathering essential details to bringing your vision to life, we handle each step efficiently, aiming for a quick, smooth launch within 2-3 weeks.

Step 1

Quick Business Insight

We begin by sending you a short questionnaire to capture the essence of your business—what you offer, your unique strengths, and more. We’ll take care of transforming your answers into impactful content.

Step 2

Design Walkthrough & Feedback

We present a first draft of your website in a screen-share meeting, guiding you through the design and gathering your input to ensure it aligns with your vision.

Step 3

Build & Launch

With your approval, we proceed with development. Once the site is ready, you’ll receive a demo link to review before we officially launch, ensuring a perfect start.
